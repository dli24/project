About Me

Pursing the path of becoming an Full-Stack Web-Developer is the most significant decision that had been ever made in my career. From college year to the first step of the competitive market, I overcome through many majors and career (ex. Mechanical Engineering, Finance,Accounting, Marketing, Sales), but none of them caught my attention. My final decision are made to pursue web-developer because this is where I found out that this career are leading into my comfort zone. The challenge coming through web-developer are really interesting and solving an challenge is really satisfying. It is not easy to know what is the best fit for me, but once web-developer caught my attention, I do not regret to move on to this path.
